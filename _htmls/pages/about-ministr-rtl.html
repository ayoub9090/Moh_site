About the Minister’s Office

The Office of the Minister provides all secretarial and administrative services with the latest advanced scientific methods; creating an appropriate environment for the Minister to carry out His Excellency's work effectively and efficiently.

Tasks and Responsibilities

  • Providing all secretarial and administrative services according to the latest scientific methods; creating an appropriate environment for the Minister to carry out His Excellency's work effectively and efficiently
  • Handling correspondence, presenting them to His Excellency, and following his directions regarding them.
  • Receiving private and confidential communications, presenting them to His Excellency and sending them according to his directions.
  • Writing letters, telegrams, and memorandums and preparing reports requested by the Minister.
  • Receiving the public and their documents, presenting them to His Excellency and following his directions regarding them.
  • Informing the concerned authorities of any written or verbal instructions and directives issued by the Minister...
